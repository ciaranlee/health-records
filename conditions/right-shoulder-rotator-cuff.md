# Right Shoulder — Rotator Cuff Injury

## Summary

Right shoulder rotator cuff injury, onset August 2025. Diagnosed by Kieran Nolan (sports physio) at Sports Med Ireland. Responding well to rehab exercises focused on shoulder stabilisation.

## Timeline

- **2025-08** — Onset of right shoulder issues
- **2025-09-19** — Assessed by Kieran Nolan at [Sports Med Ireland](https://www.sportsmedireland.ie/about); diagnosed as rotator cuff injury. See [consultation notes](../consultations/2025-09-19-kieran-nolan-right-shoulder.md).

- **~early 2026** — Fell out of rehab habit; symptoms returned
- **2026-03-12** — Restarting rehab programme

## Current Status

Shoulder is not right — improved significantly with rehab exercises but regressed after falling out of the habit. Restarting the programme from 2026-03-12.

## Management

### Rehab Programme

Prescribed by Kieran Nolan at Sports Med Ireland. Focus is on shoulder stabilisation.

#### Daily

- Shoulder holds (press-up position, lift one hand, touch opposite shoulder). 3 sets of 5 reps each side. Hold 3 seconds per rep.
- Side plank hold — 3 sets of 20 seconds per side.

#### 3x per week (Monday / Wednesday / Friday)

- Side plank with band row. 3 sets of 8 reps per side.
- Overhead carry. 15 kg weight held overhead. 3 sets of 30 steps.
- Lat pull-down with band. Start with shoulder shrug back. Pull band down, thumbs in. 3 sets of 8 reps.
- Push-up with med ball touch. At the top, lift the floor hand and touch opposite shoulder. 3 sets of 5 reps each side.

### What helps

- Consistency with the rehab exercises
- Shoulder stabilisation work generally
